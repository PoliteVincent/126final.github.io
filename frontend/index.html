UBC Coaching Services is committed to advancing the vision of UBC's organizational coaching culture with a focus on leadership, career, health and wellbeing, conflict and change. Coaching Services is for UBC faculty and staff, and includes individual, group and custom coaching partnerships. Coaching is an inquiry-based process with the coach acting as a thought-partner, through the demonstration of active listening and curiosity. Coaching supports goal-setting, accountability and action by surfacing new awareness, insights and learning. It is not counselling or therapy.
